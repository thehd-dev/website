Intro

Hayden is a software developer operating from Brisbane, Australia and has been working in the industry since he was developing websites in school. Since achieving a Bachelor of Information and Technology with distinction from Queensland University of Technology in 2011 he has acrued a wealth of knowledge and a vast set of skills and experience in the field of software development. See some of his recent work experiences here.

With a passion for software and anything tech related, Hayden frequently has multiple side projects happening as well. These passion projects sometimes become published products but not always. These projects are used to sharpen skills and explore new technologies and sometimes can be used to create passive income. See some of his recent side projects here.
