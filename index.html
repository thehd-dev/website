Intro

Hayden is a software developer based in Brisbane, Australia, with a passion for technology that began in high school. After graduating with distinction in a Bachelor of Information Technology from Queensland University of Technology in 2011, he has built a broad skill set and deep industry experience. View some of his professional work.

Outside of his professional roles, Hayden regularly explores new technologies through various side projects. Some evolve into published products, while others serve as platforms to refine skills and test ideas. Check out some of his recent projects.

If you're seeking a versatile developer with a strong technical foundation and a history of end-to-end delivery, review his skillset and get in touch about opportunities.
