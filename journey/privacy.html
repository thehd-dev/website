Permissions We Request

To deliver core features of Journey – Music Player, the app may request access to certain system permissions, depending on your device and operating system. We only request permissions that are necessary, and no personal music files or playback history are uploaded or collected by us.

Media Access

  • Used to scan, list, and play music stored locally on your device, including showing album artwork and embedded metadata.

Network & Connectivity

  • Used for features such as optional feedback, checking for updates, crash reporting (if enabled), and media casting or local file sharing.

Background Playback

  • Allows playback to continue while the app is in the background or the screen is off.

Notifications

  • Used to display media playback controls and track information in the system notification area or lock screen. You may disable these at any time in your device’s settings.
